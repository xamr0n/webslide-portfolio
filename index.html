About Me

My name is Norman and I have been a document controller for 15 years. During this time, I have gained extensive experience working on a variety of projects in different construction industries.

As a document controller, my role is to ensure that all project documents are properly managed and controlled throughout their lifecycle. This includes everything from creating and maintaining document registers to managing the review and approval process. It may not sound glamorous, but it is a critical function that keeps projects running smoothly.
